Tokita Ohma (十 (と) 鬼 (き) 蛇 (た) 王 (おう) 馬 (ま), Tokita Ōma; "Ohma Tokita"), also known as The Asura (阿 (ア) 修 (シュ) 羅 (ラ), Ashura), is a man who loves to fight. He entered into the Kengan Association, fighting for the Nogi Group. During the Kengan Annihilation Tournament, Ohma fought for Yamashita Trading Co. with Yamashita Kazuo as his employer.Ohma is a rough, yet well-meaning individual. He was initially rather arrogant, which often surfaced whenever he fought. During his fights, he was condescending and often belittled and provoked his opponents.
